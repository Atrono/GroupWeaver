## Decided

- **Execution scope for this session was report-only** - the user confirmed
  no changes should land without a separate later go-ahead.
- **If/when Tier 2 is greenlit, include items 7 (.NET analyzers) and 9
  (Dependabot) by default** rather than re-asking - the user already opted in
  to both despite their flagged real costs (one-time analyzer triage effort;
  recurring Dependabot PR-review load).
- **`tools/` stays agent-editable, not frozen** - CLAUDE.md already mandates
  `bootstrap.ps1` be kept current with every installed dependency, and
  `build.ps1` is the repo's sole extension point for new quality gates; a
  blanket write-freeze would conflict with both. A narrower "flag gate-surface
  diffs in the PR body" rule was proposed instead, held as a Tier 3 item
  pending buy-in, not applied.
