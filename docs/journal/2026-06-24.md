## Decided

- **A documented "decorative" intent does NOT exempt readable text from WCAG 1.4.3.** The mockup's
  accent-on-accent-soft DEMO badge and green-on-green-soft Read-only pill are *text*; the project's
  hard ≥4.5:1 bar (ADR-021) wins over the mockup's flourish. Deepen the ink per theme rather than
  ship sub-threshold text or claim an exemption. ADR-026 D6 reworded accordingly.
- **Explicit `Button.accent` style over `SystemAccentColor`.** Fluent derives a lighter hover from
  the accent seed that breaches 4.5:1 for white text; an explicit ramp (rest/hover/pressed all
  ≥5:1) is the accessible, controllable choice. Decorative accent (`#8B7BFF`/`#6A5CFF`) is never
  used as button text.
